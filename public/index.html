Hi, I'm Afton!

I didn't grow up loving tech. I did, however, grow up taking things apart and putting them back together just for the fun of it to see how they work.

I am a current undergraduate student studying Computer Science at Southern Illinois University-Carbondale. I am passionate about building software solutions that solve some of the world's problems! I received my Bachelor of Science in Physiology in 2019, which led me to acquire the job I have now: Microbiology Lab Manager. After 6 months of working in that role, I started programming out of curiosity and the need for a creative outlet. After creating a small web scraping program, I became enveloped in the power of software.

Since then, many things have happened, and I have matured. I have found out more about myself, my passions, and whom I want to be. I will be completing my Bachelor's in Computer Science in May of 2023. Through taking university courses and through working on my own personal projects, I have experience developing programs/software in Java, C++, JavaScript, and bash scripting in both Windows and Linux environments. I also am familiar with development tools such as git and vim. I am interested in building highly scalable and distributed systems, particularly in Java, and understanding how API's can be built to optimize the communication of data from frontend to backend. Finding my place at a company with a strong vision and a team-oriented environment is my current goal. I aspire to find a place where I can grow as a software developer, leader, team member, and teacher.

Outside of my professional life, I enjoy weightlifting, woodworking, and fishing!

I also recognize the value of a strong network, so please feel free to reach out to me @ lawverap25@gmail.com.
